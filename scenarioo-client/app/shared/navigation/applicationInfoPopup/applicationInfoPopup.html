Automated Documentation through UI Tests


Documentation is in your code and your tests.
Yes... but... you have to make it visible!

Our vision

Scenarioo helps you visualizing and documenting the functionalities of your software in a guidebook, that contains the important user interaction flows of your software, generated out of your UI tests. Each so called "User Scenario" is presented as step by step page flow with screenshots and any additional useful information that you want to provide. Using the Scenarioo web frontent everybody in your project can jump from step to step of your user scenarios almost like a Kangoroo.

This is helpfull to all kind of stakeholders in your project, such as developers, requirement engineers, testers, technical support, product owners, project leaders, business or sales representatives, customers or even users.
